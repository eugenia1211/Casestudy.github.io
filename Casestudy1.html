What if the customers complain if she/he cannot pay the water bills?

We have 2 ways to pay water bills:
- first, it can be offline, You have to go to the Administration of water bills in near area, if you still didn't find it, we can escort you to the direction.
- second, it can be online. you can go to the http://178.128.119.41/
-Enter a cluster or Region name.
-Enter your block number.
-And enter the cellphone number that was registered.
-If you have logged in to check data.
-After logging in, information will appear regarding payment details that must be paid
For payments, make sure to follow online operating hours from 07.00 WIB to 22.00 WIB.
